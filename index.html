About Us

Hair Salon K is a cozy, professional salon in Seattle, run by a husband-and-wife team. We’re all about truly understanding your style needs before we start cutting. Our lead stylist makes sure you get exactly what you envision, every time.

You’ll enjoy a comfortable, clean environment—and we always shampoo your hair before cutting to ensure a refreshed, confident look. Our focus is on quality, affordability, and personalized care for each of our clients.

We also offer a fantastic opportunity for stylists looking to build their clientele: our salon has five open chairs for rent. If you’re a talented stylist seeking a welcoming, high-traffic environment in Seattle, we invite you to join our team!
